- **Tier 1 (Verified):** Declassified government documents, court records, peer-reviewed research, official statements
- **Tier 2 (Plausible):** Investigative journalism with multiple sources, expert testimony, leaked documents with corroboration
- **Tier 3 (Unverified):** Single-source claims, anonymous testimony, circumstantial patterns without direct proof
