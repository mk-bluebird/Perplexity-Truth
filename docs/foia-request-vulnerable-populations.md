# FOIA Request Template – Vulnerable Populations (Homelessness, Substance Use, Mandated Treatment)

> PURPOSE: To understand how an agency treats unhoused people and those with substance‑use disorders, and whether “innovative” tech or behavioral programs are being applied to them.

## Request #3 – Programs Targeting Homelessness, Substance Use, and Mandated Treatment

This is a request for records under A.R.S. § 39‑121 et seq. (or applicable state law).

I request the following records, for the period **[Start Date] through [End Date]**:

1. **Program Descriptions**

   - All program descriptions, policy documents, grant applications, or internal proposals relating to:
     - Specialized units or task forces addressing **homelessness** or “quality of life” offenses;
     - Programs targeting **people with substance‑use disorders** (including opioid, methamphetamine, or polysubstance use), especially where participation is tied to diversion, probation, or reduced charges;
     - **Drug courts, mental‑health courts, or mandated treatment** programs operated or supported by [Agency Name];
     - Any “innovative,” “technology‑enabled,” or “data‑driven” initiatives involving these populations.

2. **Use of Technology and Data**

   - All records describing the use of:
     - Risk‑scoring tools, predictive analytics, or algorithmic systems to identify, classify, or prioritize interventions for individuals experiencing homelessness or substance use;
     - Electronic monitoring, wearables, mobile apps, or other devices used to track location, behavior, or compliance of participants in housing, shelter, or treatment programs;
     - Neurotechnology, digital therapeutics, or VR/AR tools specifically deployed for people in these categories.

3. **Partnerships and Contractors**

   - All MOUs, contracts, or agreements between [Agency Name] and:
     - Private contractors providing services related to homelessness, treatment, or behavioral management;
     - Universities or research institutions conducting studies on people experiencing homelessness, people with substance‑use disorders, or court‑mandated clients within [Agency Name]’s jurisdiction.

   - This includes any documentation that describes the **research aims**, data‑collection methods, consent procedures, and use of technology in such partnerships.

4. **Complaints and Oversight**

   - Any summary reports, audits, or reviews by internal oversight bodies, inspectors general, or external monitors that:
     - Evaluate the treatment of people experiencing homelessness or substance‑use disorders by [Agency Name];
     - Address complaints of harassment, excessive force, psychological abuse, or unusual technological interventions in shelters, encampments, jails, or treatment facilities.

If responsive records are held by sub‑agencies, contractors, or task forces within [Agency Name], please forward this request as necessary and indicate which entities have been tasked to respond.

I prefer to receive all responsive records in electronic form.
