## Request #2 – Sleep Deprivation, Sensory Manipulation, Behavioral Modification

This is a request for records under A.R.S. § 39‑121 et seq. (or applicable state law).

I request the following records, for the period **[Start Date] through [End Date]**:

1. **Policies and Protocols**

   - All policies, directives, guidelines, standard operating procedures, or internal memoranda that address:
     - The use of **sleep‑deprivation**, sleep‑restriction, or intentionally disruptive scheduling for individuals in custody, detention, or mandated programs;
     - The use of **constant lighting, loud or repetitive noise, alarms, or other environmental controls** that may affect sleep, perception, or mental state;
     - Any **“segregation,” “administrative segregation,” “protective custody,” or “behavioral management”** regimes that restrict sensory input (e.g., isolation, darkness, hooding) or apply persistent stimuli (e.g., constant sound);
     - The use of **psychological tactics** designed to influence or modify behavior, including but not limited to repeated “loops” of messaging, scripted verbal protocols, or automated audio/visual content.

2. **Legal Opinions and Risk Assessments**

   - All legal opinions, risk assessments, ethical reviews, or internal analyses concerning:
     - Whether any of the above practices could constitute **cruel, inhuman, or degrading treatment** or violate constitutional protections;
     - The psychological or psychiatric impact of these practices on detainees, probationers, or individuals in treatment programs;
     - Compliance with national or international standards regarding torture, psychological integrity, or neurorights.

3. **Incident Reports and Complaints**

   - All aggregate reports, audits, or summaries (with personal identifiers redacted as required by law) that:
     - Track incidents, grievances, or complaints related to sleep disruption, noise, constant illumination, sensory manipulation, or other conditions affecting mental state;
     - Describe investigations into alleged psychological harassment, intimidation, or unusual “behavior‑modification” programs in facilities operated or overseen by [Agency Name].

   - If available, anonymized statistics on:
     - Number of grievances/complaints by facility and year;
     - Outcomes of investigations (e.g., policy change, discipline, no action).

4. **Contracts for Environmental or Behavioral Systems**

   - All contracts, purchase orders, or technical documentation for systems used to:
     - Manage lighting, acoustic environments, or other environmental controls in detention, shelter, or treatment facilities;
     - Deliver automated announcements, messages, or audio/visual content aimed at compliance, behavior change, or “motivation” of residents.

For each category, if records are maintained in an electronic case‑management or facilities system, please either export the relevant records or produce documentation explaining the system fields and how responsive records may be retrieved.

I prefer electronic copies of all responsive records.
