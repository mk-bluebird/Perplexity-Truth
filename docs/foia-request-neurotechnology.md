## Request #1 – Neurotechnology and Brain‑Related Devices

This is a request for records under A.R.S. § 39‑121 et seq. (or applicable state law) and related transparency provisions.

I request the following records, for the period **[Start Date] through [End Date]**:

1. **Contracts and Agreements**

   - All contracts, purchase orders, invoices, MOUs, or other agreements between [Agency Name] and any vendor, research institution, or contractor involving:
     - Electroencephalography (EEG) devices or systems;
     - Brain–computer interfaces (BCIs);
     - Neuromodulation or neurostimulation devices;
     - Brain imaging or neural monitoring tools used outside of standard diagnostic medical practice;
     - VR/AR systems used for behavior‑modification, training, therapy, or monitoring;
     - “Digital therapeutics” or “neuro‑digital therapeutics” marketed for mental health, addiction, or behavior change.

2. **Policies, Protocols, and Training**

   - All policies, standard operating procedures, protocols, manuals, guidelines, or internal memoranda describing:
     - The intended use, deployment, or testing of any of the above technologies;
     - Consent procedures for individuals subjected to these technologies;
     - Data‑handling, retention, and sharing practices for neural or biometric data;
     - Any risk assessments, ethical reviews, or legal opinions concerning neurorights, mental privacy, or mental integrity.

   - All training materials, slide decks, presentations, or curricula provided to [Agency Name] personnel regarding:
     - Neurotechnology, EEG, BCIs, or neuromodulation;
     - VR/AR systems used to influence behavior, emotion, or cognition;
     - Use of these technologies with people experiencing homelessness, people with substance‑use disorders, or individuals in correctional or mandated‑treatment settings.

3. **Pilot Projects and Research**

   - All records describing pilot projects, trials, research collaborations, or grants involving the deployment or testing of neurotechnology, EEG‑based monitoring, VR/AR behavior‑modification systems, or digital therapeutics on:
     - Incarcerated persons;
     - Pre‑trial detainees;
     - Individuals on probation, parole, or drug‑court supervision;
     - Individuals receiving services in homeless shelters, supportive housing, or substance‑use treatment programs administered or supervised by [Agency Name].

   - This includes project descriptions, IRB approvals (if any), progress reports, evaluations, and final reports.

If any of the requested records exist only in part, or are held by sub‑units or contractors of [Agency Name], please search those locations or forward this request as appropriate. If no responsive records are found for any item, please state that explicitly in your response.

I prefer to receive these records electronically, via email or secure link, in a machine‑readable format where possible.
